## 🎯 Key Decisions Made

### Why These Colors?
- **Blue:** Trust & professionalism (essential for construction)
- **Teal:** Modern tech feel (AI-powered)
- **Orange:** Action & warmth (Indian market resonance)

### Why This Layout?
- **Split auth pages:** Common in modern SaaS, builds trust
- **Gradient hero:** Eye-catching, differentiates from competitors
- **Feature grid:** Easy scanning, clear value props
- **Generous spacing:** Premium feel, not cluttered

### Why This Typography?
- **Poppins:** Friendly but professional, works in Indian context
- **Inter:** Highly readable, excellent for body text

## 💡 Design Principles Applied

1. **Clarity First:** No confusion about what we do
2. **Mobile-First:** Indian users primarily mobile
